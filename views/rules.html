Rules

The Rules of SengGo

Objective

At the end of the game, the player with the highest score wins

The game ends when both players consecutively pass or when the board is completely filled with tokens

Gameplay

On any given turn, the player must either place a token on the board or pass

The player with BLACK tokens must play the first move

Board and Tokens

Go is played on a nxn sized board, where n must be one of the numbers: 9, 13, or 19

There must be tokens of ONLY two different colours

Tokens must be placed at the intersections of the board

An army must have at least one liberty to remain in play

Scoring

The TOTAL SCORE of a particular colour of token must be calculated by summing up the territories, captured armies, and the individual tokens of that colour on the board

Points must be scored by capturing armies or by capturing territories

One point is earned per token captured (i.e if the captured army contains 10 tokens, then the player will receive 10 points)

Since BLACK always makes the first move, the player with WHITE tokens must get bonus points due to the Komi rule

Illegal Moves

Placement of a token in a position where another token already exists

Suicide rule: placement of a token that will result in an army of the same colour having zero liberties

Ko rule: two consecutive moves that result in the prior board state (causing an infinite loop to occur)
